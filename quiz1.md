#### Is $n^2 + 2n + 6$ $\in$ $O(n^3)$

Answer: Yes!

Proof: 

- $n^2 + 2n + 6 \le n^3$
- $c = 1$
- $n_0 = 3$

|    $n$         | $n^2 + 2n + 6$ |    $n^3$       |
| -------------- | -------------- | -------------- |
|       1        |        9       |       1        |
|       2        |        14      |       8        |
|       3        |        21      |       27       |
